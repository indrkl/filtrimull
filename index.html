During 24H post-truth hackathon, team filterBubble explored many things, one of which is how google image search gives images depending on what language you use for very simple terms. This is mostly for entertainment purposes and we were unable to draw any meaningful conclusions other than it was interesting. To get the results we used google.**, ending for each country. (co.uk, de, fr etc.) and used google translate to get the appropriate words for other languages. While we had estonian, english, and somewhat german and russian skills in the team, we were completely in the blind for rest of the languages. To use this little interactive toy, choose a word and click on a language.
